Our 25-Year Journey of Excellence

For the past 25 years, Leaf Hospital has been a pillar of healthcare innovation, providing high-quality care and making strides in medical research and patient satisfaction. Here’s a look back at some of our key achievements.

1998: Founding of Leaf Hospital

Leaf Hospital opened its doors in 1998 with the vision to provide compassionate and exceptional care to the community. Our journey began with a small team of dedicated healthcare professionals.

2005: Expansion of the Facility

In 2005, Leaf Hospital expanded its facilities, adding new wings to accommodate growing patient demand. This expansion also included state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, enhancing the quality of care.

2010: Recognition for Excellence

In 2010, Leaf Hospital was awarded the "Best Hospital for Patient Care" award, a testament to our commitment to providing world-class healthcare services to our community.

2015: Launch of Specialized Cardiology Department

Recognizing the increasing need for specialized cardiac care, Leaf Hospital launched a state-of-the-art Cardiology Department, offering a wide range of heart health services including surgery, rehabilitation, and diagnostics.

2018: National Accreditation Achieved

In 2018, Leaf Hospital achieved national accreditation, solidifying our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of healthcare and operational excellence.

2023: Expansion to a Regional Medical Hub

In 2023, Leaf Hospital expanded to become a regional medical hub, offering comprehensive services ranging from general healthcare to specialized treatments in multiple fields, including oncology, orthopedics, and pediatrics.

2025: A Commitment to the Future

As we enter our 25th year, we remain dedicated to improving healthcare outcomes, embracing new medical technologies, and serving our community with compassion and care. The future is bright, and we’re ready for the next chapter.
